Grilled lobster tail 1500THB

Grilled lobster tail is a tasty seafood treat. The tail is split open and cooked on a grill until tender and juicy. This method brings out its natural sweetness and adds a smoky flavor. It's often served with melted butter and lemon for a rich and delicious meal that seafood lovers enjoy.

Foie gras 1500THB

Foie gras is a gourmet food made from the liver of ducks or geese that have been specially fattened. It's known for its rich, buttery texture and delicate flavor. Foie gras is often served as a luxurious appetizer or in fine dining dishes, where its smooth consistency and luxurious taste make it a sought-after delicacy.
